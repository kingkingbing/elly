Wonderful Princesses

Learn about these amazing princesses and their stories!

Linked to Mercury

Snow White

Snow White is kind and friendly. She lives with seven little dwarfs in a forest cottage. Her favorite color is red, and she loves to sing and clean with the animals.

She ate a poisoned apple but was saved by a prince's kiss. Snow White teaches us to be kind to everyone, even strangers.

Linked to Venus

Little Mermaid

The Little Mermaid, whose name is Ariel, lives under the sea. She loves to collect human things and dreams of walking on land. She has a beautiful voice and swims with fish friends.

She made a deal with a sea witch to get legs, but she had to give up her voice. Ariel teaches us to follow our dreams and be brave.

Back to top
Linked to Earth

Cinderella

Cinderella is kind and hardworking, even though her stepmother and stepsisters are mean to her. She loves mice and has a fairy godmother who helps her go to a ball.

She lost a glass slipper at midnight, and a prince used it to find her. Cinderella teaches us to be kind even when others are not, and that dreams can come true.

Back to top
Linked to Mars

Belle

Belle loves to read books and is very smart. She lives in a small village but dreams of adventure. She saves her father by living with a Beast in his castle.

Belle sees the good in the Beast and helps him turn back into a prince. She teaches us to look beyond appearances and love people for who they are inside.

Back to top
Linked to Jupiter

Jasmine

Jasmine is a princess from Agrabah who lives in a palace. She doesn't want to marry a prince she doesn't love, so she runs away and meets Aladdin.

She rides a magic carpet and outsmarts an evil vizier. Jasmine teaches us to be independent and follow our hearts, not just what others want.

Back to top
Linked to Saturn

Mulan

Mulan is brave and loves her family. When her father is too sick to fight in the army, she dresses as a boy and takes his place.

She shows that girls can be strong and smart. Mulan teaches us to be brave for our family and to be proud of who we are.

Back to top
Linked to Uranus

Thumblina

Thumblina is very small, about the size of a thumb. She is born from a flower and has many adventures, meeting frogs, beetles, and birds.

She wants to find a home where she belongs. Thumblina teaches us that big things can come in small packages, and to never give up on finding happiness.

Linked to Neptune

Aurora

Aurora, also called Sleeping Beauty, is put under a spell as a baby to prick her finger on a spindle and fall asleep for 100 years.

A prince finds her and wakes her with a kiss. Aurora teaches us that true love can overcome any obstacle, and patience is a virtue.
